Email voice

Claude writes every Day 1 email for you, personalised per lead. This page is where you shape the tone.
How this works. Every time the pipeline runs, Claude analyses each lead and writes a bespoke cold email using their real stats (reviews, engagement, competitors). You don't need to write templates — you just tell Claude how you want the emails to sound.

The guidance below is prepended to Claude's writing instructions. Leave blank to use the built-in premium-outreach voice.

About your business

Gives Claude the facts about what you're pitching. Include your offer, price range, ideal-client profile, and who you're not right for.

Voice & tone

Tone, phrasing preferences, hard rules. Different from business facts above — this shapes how it sounds, not what it says.
Cancel
